Fortnite

Fortnite is an online video game developed by Epic Games and released in 2017.
It is available in three distinct game mode versions that otherwise share the same general gameplay and game engine: Fortnite Battle Royale, a free-to-play battle royale game in which up to 100 players fight to be the last person standing; Fortnite: Save the World,
a cooperative hybrid tower defense-shooter and survival game in which up to four players
fight off zombie-like creatures and defend objects with traps and fortifications they can build
