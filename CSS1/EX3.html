Maccabi Tel Aviv is the only football team that has never been relegated from the Israeli Premier League.
Founded in 1906 in Jaffa as the HaRishon Le Zion-Yafo Association, it is the oldest and most decorated football club in Israel.
In 1922, it became the first Jewish football club to participate in local competitions. The meaning of the name Maccabi is 'there is no one like you among the gods'.
